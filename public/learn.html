What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is digital money that operates on blockchain technology - a decentralized network that records and verifies transactions without banks or central authorities.

Popular cryptocurrencies include Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), BNB, Solana (SOL), and Tron (TRX).

Key Insight: Cryptocurrencies are volatile but offer opportunities for significant returns through strategic trading.

Crypto Glossary

Blockchain

A distributed digital ledger that records transactions across many computers

Altcoin

Any cryptocurrency other than Bitcoin

HODL

Hold On for Dear Life - a strategy of holding crypto long-term

Market Cap

Total value of a cryptocurrency = price multiplied by circulating supply

Volatility

Degree of price fluctuation - high in crypto markets

Whale

An investor with large amounts of cryptocurrency
