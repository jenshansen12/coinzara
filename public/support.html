Frequently Asked Questions

How do I deposit funds?
1. Navigate to Deposit page
2. Select your network (BTC, ETH, BNB, SOL, TRON)
3. Copy the deposit address
4. Send crypto from your wallet
5. Balance credits within 15-30 minutes
How do I withdraw funds?
1. Go to Withdraw page
2. Select network and enter wallet address
3. Enter amount and submit
4. Processed within 24-48 hours
How does AI trading work?
Our AI bot trades 24/7 using advanced algorithms. Profits are added to your balance automatically.
How do referral codes work?
Share your unique referral code. When friends sign up and deposit, you earn bonus credits.
Is there a fee on AI-generated profits?
Yes, Coinzara charges a 10% fee on all AI-generated profits. For example, if the AI bot generates $100 in profit, a $10 fee will be applied. Your available balance will increase by $90, but your dashboard will still show the full $100 profit for transparency. This fee is clearly displayed in the withdrawal breakdown section.
